# Quantum Computing Learning Exercises

This folder contains hands-on exercises and small challenge solutions designed to strengthen **core quantum computing concepts**.  
All code is exploratory and focused on learning, not deployment.

---

## Contents

- **Basic Qiskit Practice** – Setting up qubits, gates, and measurements in simulation.
- **Gate Experiments** – Hands-on practice with single- and multi-qubit gates.
- **Challenge Solutions** – Solutions to small quantum tasks and exercises.

---

## Learning Goals

- Understand qubit manipulation and gate operations.
- Explore circuit design and simulation.
- Solve small quantum computing challenges to build algorithmic thinking.
- Develop disciplined experimentation habits.
